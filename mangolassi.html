Lassi is a traditional yogurt-based drink, originating from the Punjab district in India. Popular flavors of lassi include sweet lassi, salty lassi, and mango lassi. I grew up drinking mango lassi. My middle-school friends and classmates begged me to bring the beverage to class parties and events. And so it became my mom's famous secret recipe in the community...

Ingredients
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 cup of canned mango pulp
  • 4 teaspoons honey or sugar, more or less to taste
  • 1/2 lemon squeezed
  • Ice (optional)
Procedure
  1. Put mango pulp, yogurt, milk, sugar and lemon into a blender and blend for 2 minutes.
  2. If you want a more milkshake consistency and it's a hot day, either blend in some ice as well or serve over ice cubes.
