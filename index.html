Welcome to Chef Yusra Company! I stole my mom's cookbook to share popular everyday recipes from my South Asian household with you all, combining my love for cooking and Indian culture. While these delicacies may be enjoyed at Indian restaurants, they can also be made at home with only a few ingredients. Posted are recipes for beverages, appetizers, entrees, and desserts!
