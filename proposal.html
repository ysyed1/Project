Yusra's Project Proposal

What is your website about?
I plan to create a website including popular everyday recipes from my South Asian household. In an effort to combine my hobby of cooking and share my Indian culture, I will post one recipe for each category: beverage, appetizer, main dish, and dessert (4 pages of content).
What is the objective of your website? Why would this site be important?
I hope to expose others to the flavors of the South Asian cuisine. While these delicacies may be enjoyed at Indian restaurants, they can also be made at home with only a few ingredients! For example, turmeric (referred to as haldi in Urdu) is an Indian spice used in countless South Asian recipes and beauty routines; however, Americans are only familiar with the spice in turmeric lattes at coffee shops. In this time of social distancing, my site could help others develop a new hobby & a love for Indian spices & flavors.
Who is your target audience set?
My target audience is female high school and college age students, with a love for cooking or just looking for a way to occupy their time.
What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
As mentioned briefly above, for the 4 pages of content, I will share one recipe for each category: beverage, appetizer, main dish, and dessert. As of now, I plan to feature Mango Lassi, Bhel Puri, Shahi Tukra, and Chicken Biryani. Recipes will include serving size, a list of ingredients, prodecures, and images. Tips for best outcome will also be provided from expert chef (my mother!).
